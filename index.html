FAQ

Questions, answered.

How long does setup take?

About 30 minutes. We configure your account, sync your client list, and your team can start catalogueing on their next visit. No infrastructure to install.

Do my cleaners need training?

If they can take a photo with a smartphone, they can use SmartStorageBox. Our AI handles all the identification and cataloguing. We provide a short onboarding guide for your team, typically under 20 minutes.

How much can I charge clients?

Most partners charge between $50 and $200 per session depending on scope. Some bundle it into premium recurring packages. The pricing is entirely yours, and SmartStorageBox is your tool to deploy.

Does this integrate with my existing tools?

Yes. SmartStorageBox has native integrations with HubSpot, Jobber, and Housecall Pro. We also support webhooks and API access for custom integrations.

Is client data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored on SOC 2 compliant infrastructure, and only accessible by the homeowner and authorized service providers. We never sell or share client data.
